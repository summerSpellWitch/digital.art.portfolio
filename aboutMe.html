About Me

My name is Jerrod Reyes. I enjoy playing video games, watching anime, and worldbuilding in my free time. I heavily prefer fiction over non-fiction and fantasy over sci-fi. I quite enjoy the anime art style and aesthetic. Anime has been a pretty big factor / influence on my life and it shows in some of my artwork. Video games have also had a major influence on my life. I enjoy creating things, especially worldbuilding. It's nice and oddly freeing in a way to have an entire world for you to change and tweak to your whims and wishes. When I'm not playing games, watching anime or assuming the role of a curator for my fictional world I'm thinking about story ideas, interesting / unusual concepts and compelling characters that I can add to my worldbuilding project.
